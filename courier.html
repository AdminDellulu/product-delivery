02 / Couriers & products

Each carrier is a panel: its scorecard — delivery rate vs target, RTO, speed, breach, freight, NDR, Prepaid-vs-COD, zone speed — sits at the top, with every product it moved as a horizontal card below. Key numbers also show in the collapsed header. Click a carrier to open, a product to expand. Filter to one product to compare it across carriers.

03 / Pincode RTO blacklist — confidence-ranked

Ranked by a statistical floor (Wilson 95% lower bound), not raw RTO% — so an 11-order pincode at 54% can't outrank one with stronger evidence. Decide on the whole row, not RTO% alone: Total & Delivered show what you'd lose by blocking; COD-RTO% against the national prepaid baseline (~2%) shows whether COD is the cause; the Action column recommends the surgical fix — usually COD→prepaid-only, which stops the RTO bleed while keeping the prepaid orders. Click any column header to sort (click again to reverse).
