11 / Stuck orders — placed 4+ days ago, not yet in transit
METHODOLOGY. Unique order numbers, not line items. Delivery rate = Delivered ÷ Concluded; Concluded = Delivered + all RTO states. In-flight & cancelled excluded. Realised revenue = Order Total of Delivered; GMV = all orders. Freight uses billed Total Freight else accrued Unbilled; RTO reverse cost estimated at 2× forward. NDR entrants = 2+ attempts; recovery = delivered ÷ (delivered + RTO). Stuck = age ≥ 4 d, still Manifested/Picked Up. Tiers: T1 metros+NCR, T2 large cities, T3 rest. —
